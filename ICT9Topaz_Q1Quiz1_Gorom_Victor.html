The Book of Life

In the glorious town of San Angel, Manolo, Maria, and Joaquin has been friends since they were kids. Althought their paths took different turns — Maria was sent to Europe by her father, Manolo studied to become a bullfighter, and Joaquin joined the military of San Angel — there is still one thing that stays the same: Manolo and Joaquin wants to marry Maria.


The Main Characters

  • Manolo Sanchez(Diego Luna)
    		•

    Manolo is the son of Carlos Sanchez and Carmen Sanchez. He is a guitarrista but his father expects him to be a bullfighter just like their ancestors. He's torn between following the expectations of his family and following his heart.
  • Joaquin Mondragon Jr.(Channing Tatum)
    		•

    Joaquin is the son of the San Angel town hero, Joaquin Mondragon Sr. He joined the town military under General Posada's training. He was given the Medal of Everlasting life by Xibalba that provides the wearer invincibility.
  • Maria Posada(Zoe Saldana)
    		•

    Maria is the only child and hija of General Posada. She was sent to Europe when she was 10 so she would be more formal and lady-like. She returned to San Angel 10 years later, just in time for Manolo's first bullfight.
